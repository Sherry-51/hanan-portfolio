About Me

3+ years of TikTok expertise, one mission: helping brands go viral and scale to 7-figures.

My Journey

I started my TikTok journey in 2022 when the platform was just starting to take off. While others were focused on dancing videos, I saw the future: brand-owned channels, data-driven content, and authentic influencer marketing.

Over the past 3+ years, I've generated over 5+ million views, managed thousands in ad spend, and helped brands grow from unknown startups to viral sensations. But more importantly, I've learned that there's no substitute for hands-on expertise.

"Agencies will promise you virality. I promise you a strategy, a brand, and results you can measure."

The TikTok landscape is always changing. What worked 6 months ago doesn't work today. I'm constantly testing, learning, and adapting my strategies to stay ahead of the algorithm and trends.

  • Transparency — You'll always know exactly what's happening with your account
